SVG to PNG Favicon Converter

This tool generates PNG versions of your favicon.

Instructions:

  1. The SVG favicon will be loaded automatically
  2. The PNGs will be drawn on the canvases below
  3. Right-click each canvas and select "Save Image As..." to download
  4. Save as "favicon-16x16.png" and "favicon-32x32.png" in your public folder
