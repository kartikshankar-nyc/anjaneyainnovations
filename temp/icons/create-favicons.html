SVG to PNG Favicon Generator

This tool generates PNG favicons in 16x16 and 32x32 sizes from your SVG favicon.

Instructions:

  1. The images will be drawn on the canvases below
  2. Right-click each image and select "Save Image As..."
  3. Save as "favicon-16x16.png" and "favicon-32x32.png" in your public folder

32x32 favicon:

Right-click and save as "favicon-32x32.png"

16x16 favicon:

Right-click and save as "favicon-16x16.png"
